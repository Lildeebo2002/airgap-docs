This guide will explore how you can set up Rabby Wallet as a watch-only wallet with AirGap Vault.

Rabby Wallet is a game-changing non-custodial browser extension wallet for Defi users to access over 37 blockchains.

If you already have Rabby installed, you can skip the next step and proceed to pair Rabby with AirGap Vault.

## 1. Installation

On your PC, visit the [Rabby wallet website](https://rabby.io/) , click on "Download for chrome" or "Download for Brave" and follow the instructions for your browser.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby01.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Click the “Download for Chrome” or “Download for Brave” button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby02.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Confirm the installation by clicking on "Add extension" from the popup.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby03.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Your browser will then download and automatically install the Rabby Wallet extension.

You will get a confirmation popup after completing this step.

## 2. Set up Rabby Wallet and pair it with AirGap Vault.

Launch Rabby Wallet and click the Get Started button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby04.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Specify a password, confirm it and click the next button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby05.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Click AirGap Wallet

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby06.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Open the Vault and create an ETH account.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/rabby/Rabby09.jpg"} />

Select the ETH account

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/rabby/Rabby10.jpg"} />

Select "Rabby"

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/rabby/Rabby11.jpg"} />

Scan the QR code on Rabby Wallet.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby07.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Select the address you want and click the next button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby08.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

You should get a successfully imported message

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby12.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

## 2. Sending transaction Using Rabby Wallet and AirGap Vault

In Rabby Wallet, click "Send" button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby13.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Input your address, Select the blockchain, token/coin, specify the address and amount.
Click the send button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby14.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Click the proceed button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby15.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Open the Vault and scan the QR code.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/rabby/Rabby16.jpg"} />

Click the sign transaction button

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/rabby/Rabby17.jpg"} />

Select QR code signing.

<IPhoneWrapper src={"/img/rabby/Rabby18.jpg"} />

Go to Rabby Wallet and click the "Get Signature" button.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby19.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

Scan the QR-code from the Vault using Rabby Wallet.

<BrowserWindow url="extension://rabby">

<DynamicImage src="/img/rabby/Rabby20.png" alt="Rabby Wallet" />

</BrowserWindow>

You should get a transaction completed message from Rabby wallet at the bottom right corner of your browser.
