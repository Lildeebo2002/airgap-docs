AirGap enables users to exchange their digital assets with ease. This is achieve through our built-in exchange, which provides users with access to a range of trading pairs and liquidity options.

The AirGap exchange is powered by a combination of Quipuswap, Plenty, Sirius DEX, ChangeNow, and Changelly. This ensures that users can access a wide range of cryptocurrency assets and trade them seamlessly within the AirGap wallet. With these powerful APIs, AirGap provides users with access to deep liquidity, competitive pricing, On & Off ramp trading across a wide range of trading pairs.

### Quipuswap

[Quipuswap](https://quipuswap.com/) is a decentralized exchange (DEX) that enables users to trade their Tez (XTZ), FA1.2 and FA2 tokens in a trustless and decentralized manner. By leveraging the power of smart contracts on the Tezos network, Quipuswap enables users to trade their tokens without the need for a centralized intermediary. This makes it a highly secure and reliable option for trading tokens on the tezos blockchain.

### Plenty

[Plenty](https://plentydefi.com/) is also decentralized exchange that operates on the Tezos blockchain. As a DEX, it enables users to trade their Tez (XTZ), FA1.2 and FA2 tokens in a trustless and decentralized manner. By using Plenty, users can trade their tokens without the need for a centralized intermediary, which provides them with greater security and control over their assets.

### Sirius DEX

[Sirius DEX](https://siriustoken.io/) is another decentralized exchange that operates on the Tezos blockchain. Like Plenty, Sirius DEX enables users to trade their Tezos-based tokens in a trustless and decentralized manner. Sirius DEX is suitable and ideal for swaping between tzBTC and Tez (XTZ).

### ChangeNow

[ChangeNow](https://changenow.io/) API is a popular cryptocurrency exchange aggregator that provides users with access to a wide range of trading pairs and liquidity options. By aggregating liquidity from multiple exchanges, ChangeNow API enables users to access the best pricing and deepest liquidity across a range of cryptocurrency assets.

### Changelly

[Changelly](https://changelly.com/) API is another popular cryptocurrency exchange aggregator that provides users with access to a range of trading pairs and liquidity options. Like ChangeNow API, Changelly API aggregates liquidity from multiple exchanges to provide users with access to the best pricing and deepest liquidity across a range of cryptocurrency assets.

### Mt Pelerin

[Mt Pelerin](https://www.mtpelerin.com/) API acts as a bridge between blockchain and traditional finance systems, it provide users seamless ability to move in and out of crypto directly within their wallet, without relying on centralized exchanges.

By leveraging these six powerful APIs, AirGap provides users with a seamless and reliable trading experience within the AirGap wallet. Users can easily swap their cryptocurrencies using the AirGap exchanges, moves funds On & Off ramp easily. This ensures that users can trade their digital assets with confidence, knowing that their funds are always secure and under their control.
