AirGap supports non-fungible tokens (NFTs) on the Tezos blockchain. With AirGap, users can easily store, visualize, manage, and transfer their Tezos-based NFTs in a secure and user-friendly way.

AirGap's support for Tezos-based NFTs includes offline security of your private key to ensure the security of your funds. The intuitive interface makes it easy for users to interact with their NFTs on the Tezos blockchain, with streamlined processes for transferring NFTs.

Overall, AirGap's support for NFTs on the Tezos blockchain provides a comprehensive and secure solution for managing digital assets.

To learn how to manage your NFTs in AirGap visit [here](https://medium.com/airgap-it/airgap-supports-tezos-based-nfts-9ec9403faa5f)
