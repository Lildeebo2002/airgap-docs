AirGap Wallet is a companion app for AirGap Vault. It creates and broadcasts transactions, shows the portfolio overview and displays NFTs.

### What can you do on AirGap Wallet?

View and create transactions, manage a portfolio of multi-chain coins and tokens, and Tezos NFTs.

#### AirGap Wallet supports the following blockchains:

- Bitcoin (BTC - Legacy & SegWit)
- Ethereum (ETH & ERC20)
- Tezos(XTZ & FA1 & FA1.2 & NFTs)
- Shielded Tezos
- Polkadot (DOT)
- Kusama (KSM)
- Cosmos (ATOM)
- Aeternity (AE)
- Groestlcoin (GRS)
- Moonriver (MOVR)
- Moonbeam (GLMR)
- Astar (ASTR)
- Shiden (SDN)
- Internet Computer (ICP)

And more blockchains to come soon.

#### NFTs

AirGap Wallet provides safe and straightforward interaction with Tezos based NFTs by using the [objkt](https://objkt.com/) API.

#### Access DApps

You can access the most popular DApps with AirGap using our [WalletConnect](https://walletconnect.com/) feature for ETH and [Beacon](https://www.walletbeacon.io/) for Tezos.

#### In-app exchange

The wallet offers an Interface to exchange supported coins and tokens within AirGap. You don't need to move your coins or tokens out of AirGap to exchange them.

The exchange feature offers centralized and decentralized methods of swapping. The CEX uses either ChangeNow or Changelly APIs for seamless swapping of multiple coins. The DEX uses Quipuswap for safe and easy swapping of Tezos FA1 and FA1.2 tokens.
